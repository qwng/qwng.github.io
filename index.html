About Me

I am a final year PhD candidate of Department of Statistics at Purdue University. My research interest lies in the area of Bayesian nonparametrics, machine learning and statistical genetics. My advisor is Prof. Vinayak Rao. Currently my research focuses on developing a novel algorithm for exact simulation of stochastic differential equations via auxiliary variable Gibbs sampler that can improve the sample generating efficiency in orders of magnitude than existing methods.
